Polly’s Research Homepage¶

Greetings! I am a neuroscientist with expertise in imaging.

Currently, I provide operational support for genomic medicine programs at the Veterans Health Administration. I am also involved in the development of quantitative imaging pipelines for lung cancer.

Previously, my work involved mapping human brain function. This includes studies on somatosensation, plasticity, visual imagery (3D vs. 2D environments), language and memory.

These pages provide examples of my current and past imaging work. They also contain general information on brain imaging techniques as well as links to my CV and publications.

This site is still under construction and works best with Chrome, Firefox or Safari.
